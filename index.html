About Me

Saleem Alharir is a highly qualified technology professional with a strong academic background in the fields of information technology engineering, technology management, innovation, and electrical and computer engineering. He received a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology Engineering from Tartous University in Syria, followed by a Master's degree in Technology Management and Innovation from the University of Guanajuato in Mexico. He also holds a Master's degree of Science in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Arizona in the United States. Saleem's research areas of interest include machine learning and networking, and he has made significant contributions to these fields through his research and academic work. With his extensive education and expertise, Saleem Alharir is well-equipped to take on challenging roles in the technology industry, and he is committed to advancing the field through innovation and excellence.
